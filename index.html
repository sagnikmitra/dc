Musings by Debraj

Welcome to my portfolio! My name is Debraj, and I am an artist who loves to create sketches and a computer science professor. I believe that both art and computer science are creative endeavors that require a keen eye for detail and a deep understanding of the underlying principles. As an artist, I have always been fascinated by the power of sketches to capture the essence of a subject with just a few lines. My work is inspired by the beauty of nature, the complexity of human emotions, and the intricate interplay of light and shadow.
